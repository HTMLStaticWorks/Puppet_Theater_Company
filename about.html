Behind the Strings

Discover the history, the family, and the magic that drives every performance at the PUPPET Mansion.

Est. 1994

It Began in a
Small Dusty Attic

Puppetry wasn't our business; it was our dream. Starting with three hand-sewn marionettes and a makeshift cardboard theater, we began telling stories that children in our neighborhood would never forget.

"We didn't just want to move dolls; we wanted to move hearts." - The Founders

Meet the Wooden Family

Our troupe consists of over 50 resident marionettes, each with their own biography, personality quirk, and wardrobe. From 'Old Silas,' our 30-year-old storyteller, to 'Spark,' the mischievous fire-fly.

A Vision of the Next Act

We are blending the ancient art of puppetry with 21st-century magic. From holographic stages to interactive digital-string tech, the future of PUPPET is brighter than ever.
